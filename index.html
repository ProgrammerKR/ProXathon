About ProXathon

ProXathon is a premier solo hackathon experience, hosted by ProXentix, designed exclusively for individual creators ready to take their ideas to the next level. It empowers passionate developers to build, innovate, and solve real-world problems—independently.

  • Solo participation for individual innovators
  • Push your limits with creative challenges
  • Digital certificates & social media recognition
  • Top performers receive 1st, 2nd & 3rd place awards
  • Opportunity to showcase your work globally
